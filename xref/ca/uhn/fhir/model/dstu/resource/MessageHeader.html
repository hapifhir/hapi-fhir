69   * (A resource that describes a message that is exchanged between systems)
70   *
71   * <p>
72   * <b>Definition:</b>
73   * The header for a message exchange that is either requesting or responding to an action. 74   * </p> 
75   *
76   * <p>
77   * <b>Requirements:</b>

84   * </p>
