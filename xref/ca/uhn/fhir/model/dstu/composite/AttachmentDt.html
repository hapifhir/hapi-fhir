53  /**
54   * HAPI/FHIR <b>AttachmentDt</b> Datatype
55   * (Content in a format defined elsewhere)
56   *
57   * <p>
58   * <b>Definition:</b>
59   * For referring to data content defined in other formats.
60   * </p> 
61   *
62   * <p>
63   * <b>Requirements:</b>
64   * Many models need to include data defined in other specifications that is complex and opaque to the healthcare model. This includes documents, media recordings, structured data, etc.
65   * </p>
